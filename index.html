Find out what your Akan name is! :)

Akan names are derived from the Ghanaian culture. That's ecause the Akan people often name their children after the day of the week they were born. Depending on the gender, male and female names are different. Below are the names for both genders.


Female Akan Names

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

Male Akan Names

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
